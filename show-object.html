Recommended places to go with your pet

Restaurant
Barking dog
1678 3rd Ave, New York

A place that rewards you for bringing your dog. This place has a ton of seats outside for a Sunday brunch! Excellent service all around!

Groomer
WOWOW Grooming
3714 N Figueroa St, Los Angeles

This place is awesome! The staff honestly handles all pets as if they are their own. The facility was well maintained, the staff used great products and the prices are super affordable. My dogs and I loved the experience!! We will definitely be returning :)
